Why We Focus on Edelman Court

Los Angeles County's Dependency System Is Unlike Any Other

Edelman Children's Court in Monterey Park is the busiest juvenile dependency courthouse in the United States. Practicing effectively there requires specific knowledge that most attorneys — including many who handle CPS cases — simply do not have.

🏛️

Courthouse-Specific Experience

Judges and commissioners at Edelman Court develop specific expectations about case presentation, evidence, and procedure. An attorney who appears there regularly understands these expectations and uses them to advocate more effectively for clients.

🏢

DCFS Knowledge

DCFS is the largest child welfare agency in the U.S. We understand how it investigates cases, builds petitions, and operates — including where its cases are vulnerable and how to challenge them effectively.

⚖️

County Counsel Opposition

DCFS is represented at Edelman Court by the LA County Counsel's office — experienced litigators who know the system inside and out. Our clients deserve equally experienced representation on their side.

📚

Dependency-Focused Practice

Juvenile dependency is not a side practice for us. It is governed by California Welfare and Institutions Code §§ 300–366.26 — a highly specialized statutory framework that requires dedicated expertise.

🔗

Criminal Defense Integration

When dependency allegations overlap with criminal exposure — which is common in child abuse cases — we handle both proceedings in coordination, ensuring nothing said in dependency court undermines the criminal defense.

🌍

Serving All of Southern California

While we focus on Edelman Court and Los Angeles County, we serve families throughout Southern California, including Orange County, Riverside County, and San Bernardino County.
