About Attorney Abuershaid

Mohammad "Mo" Abuershaid is the founding attorney of ALL Trial Lawyers and Abuershaid Law, APC — a Southern California law firm dedicated to aggressive legal defense for parents and families facing government intervention in their family life. He has built a practice focused on the areas of juvenile dependency, DCFS defense, criminal defense, and personal injury, representing clients throughout Los Angeles County, Orange County, Riverside County, and beyond.

Attorney Abuershaid appears regularly at Edmund D. Edelman Children's Court in Monterey Park — the busiest juvenile dependency courthouse in the United States. His experience in this specific courthouse means he understands the judicial officers, the procedures, and the strategies that produce results for families who find themselves facing DCFS.

His firm has represented thousands of clients across Southern California — parents who have been falsely accused, parents who are fighting for reunification with their children, and parents who need an aggressive defender against DCFS in a system that can feel overwhelming and impossible to navigate alone.

Why He Does This Work

Attorney Abuershaid has spoken publicly about the human dimension of juvenile dependency work — the stakes that parents face, the speed with which DCFS can act, and the enormous difference that experienced legal representation makes at every stage of the process. He approaches every family's case as a unique situation that deserves thorough preparation, honest counsel, and relentless advocacy.

He treats families the way he would want his own family treated: with clarity, respect, and the full force of legal expertise behind every argument and every hearing.
